# Nat 20 Labs: Growth & SEO Roadmap

This document outlines the remaining strategic and technical steps to launch and scale **Nat 20 Labs** once your Google Workspace is active.

## 1. Analytics & Tracking (Pending Google Workspace)
*   **Google Search Console:** Essential for SEO. It will tell us which UK-based search terms are leading people to the site.
*   **Google Analytics 4 (GA4):** To track user behavior, specifically which "Insights" posts are keeping people engaged.
*   **Google Tag Manager:** For easier management of tracking pixels (like LinkedIn or Meta) in the future.

## 2. Lead Generation: The "Free Technical Audit"
*   **Concept:** A dedicated section or modal offering a "Free 15-Minute SEO & Performance Roll."
*   **Value:** Captures leads by providing immediate value (showing them why their current site is slow).
*   **Implementation:** Add a contact form specifically for "Audit Requests."

## 3. Local SEO & Authority (UK Market)
*   **Google Business Profile:** Create this to appear in local Map Packs. Use your London/UK address.
*   **UK Directory Citations:**
    *   FreeIndex.co.uk
    *   Yell.com
    *   Clutch.co (Highly recommended for B2B/Agencies)
*   **Backlink Strategy:** Focus on `.uk` domains to solidify your position in the UK search results.

## 4. Content & Social Strategy
*   **Case Study Deep-Dives:** As soon as a client project is completed, expand the "Work" section from placeholders to full "Problem -> Solution -> Result" pages.
*   **LinkedIn Presence:** Share lighthouse scores and technical wins. Tag your posts with `#UKBusiness` and `#TechnicalSEO`.

## 5. Technical Maintenance
*   **Sitemap Generation:** Once the site has 5+ blog posts, we should implement an automated `sitemap.xml` generator.
*   **Image Optimization:** As you add real portfolio images, ensure they are in `.webp` or `.avif` format for maximum speed.
